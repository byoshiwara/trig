Reciprocal Trigonometric Ratios.

If \(\theta\) is one of the acute angles in a right triangle,
\begin{align*} \blert{\sec (\theta)} \amp = \blert{\dfrac{\text{hypotenuse}}{\text{adjacent}}}\\ \blert{\csc (\theta)} \amp = \blert{\dfrac{\text{hypotenuse}}{\text{opposite}}}\\ \blert{\cot (\theta)} \amp = \blert{\dfrac{\text{adjacent}}{\text{opposite}}} \end{align*}
