Sum and Difference Identities for Tangent.

\begin{equation*} \blert{\tan (\alpha + \beta) = \dfrac{\tan (\alpha) + \tan (\beta)}{1 - \tan (\alpha) \tan (\beta)}} \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} \blert{\tan (\alpha - \beta) = \dfrac{\tan (\alpha) - \tan (\beta)}{1 + \tan (\alpha) \tan (\beta)}} \end{equation*}
