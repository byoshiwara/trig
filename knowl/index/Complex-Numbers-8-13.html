Quotient of Complex Numbers.

The quotient of two complex numbers \(~z_1=a+bi~\) and \(~z_2=c+di~\) is
\begin{align*} \blert{\dfrac{z_1}{z_2}} \amp = \blert{\dfrac{a+bi}{c+di}}\\ \amp = \blert{\dfrac{a+bi}{c+di} \cdot \dfrac{c-di}{c-di} = \dfrac{ac+bd}{c^2+d^2} + \dfrac{bc-ad}{c^2+d^2}i} \end{align*}
