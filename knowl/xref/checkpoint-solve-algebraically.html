Checkpoint 5.32.

Solve the equation \(~~4 \cos(C) - 6 = -3\text{,}\) for \(0\degree \le C\le 360\degree\text{.}\) Round your solutions to three decimal places.
Answer.
We isolate the trig ratio to find \(\cos(C) = \frac{3}{4}\text{,}\) and \(\cos^{-1} (\frac{3}{4}) = 41.4\degree\text{.}\) The second solution lies in the fourthe quadrant at \(360\degree - 41.4\degree = 318.6\degree\text{.}\)
