Raising to a power is the inverse operation for extracting roots; that is,

\begin{equation*} \boxed{\hphantom{0000} \left(\sqrt[n]{a} \right)^n =a \hphantom{0000}} \end{equation*}

as long as \(\sqrt[n]{a} \) is a real number. For example,

\begin{equation*} \left(\sqrt[4]{16} \right)^4= 2^4 = 16,~~~\text{ and }~~~\left(\sqrt[3]{-125} \right)^3= (-5)^3 = -125 \end{equation*} in-context
