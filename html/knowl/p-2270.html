We can also use the distributive law to multiply binomials that include trig ratios. You may have used the acronym \(\blert{FOIL}\) to remember the four multiplications in a product of binomials: \(\blert{F}\)irst terms, \(\blert{O}\)utside terms, \(\blert{I}\)nside terms, and \(\blert{L}\)ast terms.
