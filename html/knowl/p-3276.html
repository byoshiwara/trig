Multiply the first fraction by the reciprocal of the second fraction.

\begin{align*} \frac{x^2-1}{x+3}\div\frac{x^2-x-2}{x^2+5x+6} \amp =\frac{x^2-1}{x+3}\cdot\frac{x^2+5x+6}{x^2-x-2} \amp\amp\text{Factor.}\\ \amp = \frac{(x-1)(\cancel{x+1})}{\bcancel{x+3}}\cdot\frac{(\bcancel{x+3})(x+2)}{(\cancel{x+1})(x-2)}\\ \amp = \frac{(x-1)(x+2)}{x-2} \end{align*} in-context
