To divide two algebraic fractions we multiply the first fraction by the reciprocal of the second fraction. For example,

\begin{align*} \frac{2x^3}{3y}\div \frac{4x}{5y^2}\amp = \frac{2x^3}{3y}\cdot \frac{5y^2}{4x}\\ \amp = \frac{\bcancel{2x}\cdot x^2}{3\cancel{y}}\cdot \frac{5y\cdot\cancel{y}}{2\cdot \bcancel{2x}}=\frac{5x^2y}{6} \end{align*} in-context
